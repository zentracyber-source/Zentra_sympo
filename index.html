About the Symposium

Zentra’25 – An Annual Tech Extravaganza by the Cyber Department in KLNCE

The Department of Cyber Security proudly presents Zentra’25, the annual symposium that brings together aspiring innovators, young developers, problem-solvers, and creative thinkers from diverse domains of Engineering. Zentra is a celebration of technology, innovation, and collaboration, providing students a platform to showcase their skills, compete with like-minded peers, and gain insights from industry professionals.

Purpose of Zentra’25

In an age where technology is rapidly shaping the world, students need opportunities that go beyond classroom learning. Zentra’25 is designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world problem-solving. It is a forum where students can apply their theoretical learning in practical contexts, collaborate in teams, and demonstrate their ability to think critically under time-bound challenges.

Explore cutting-edge technologies in coding, web design, and cybersecurity. Recognize both technical and non-technical talents. Network with peers, alumni, faculty, and industry experts. Drive innovation-focused projects that benefit society.

What Makes Zentra’25 Special?

Zentra’25 balances competitive technical events and engaging non-technical activities. Technical sessions enhance analytical and coding strengths, while non-technical events nurture creativity, teamwork, and quick thinking.

The event believes every student has a unique contribution—whether it’s debugging code, cracking CTF challenges, presenting cybersecurity research, or energizing the crowd through quizzes and fun challenges.

Purpl3 OP5 CTF 2025 - Where Red Meets Blue

Description:

Welcome to Purple Ops CTF, a one-of-a-kind cybersecurity competition designed to test your skills across both offensive (Red Team) and defensive (Blue Team) domains.

This isn’t your typical CTF—every round is crafted to simulate real-world challenges that security professionals face daily. From spotting traces in logs to tearing apart malware, from dissecting network traffic to full-blown system exploitation—you’ll face it all here.

Event Rounds:

Round 1-Jeopardy-style challenges:

FOR3N6: Case Study Presentation

Description:

Showcase your analytical and investigative skills by solving real-world cyber forensic cases. Present your findings with clarity, evidence, and strategy. A platform to test technical knowledge, reasoning, and presentation skills in the world of digital forensics.

Instruction:

  • Pair of Participants: Each team will consists of two participants.
  • Presentation Topics: Analysis must be focused on cybersecurity breaches.
  • Analysis Time Period:Confirm that the subject mater is relevant to the present timeframe.
  • Allocation Duration: The presentation should be completed within 5 -7 Minutes
  • Presentation deck: Each team should prepare 6 - 7 Slides.

The Recommended Outline For This Presenation:

  • Title: The name of the case.
  • Incident Overview: A summary of what occured.
  • Cause/Sequence: Explanation of how it unfolded / initiated.
  • Impact/Consequences: The effects or impact of the incident.
  • Resolution/Steps Taken: The actions taken to address the issue.
  • Preventive Measures & Key Insights: Risk reduction steps and lessons learned.

Requirements:

  • PPT file in USB or Send to Email.

Subway Actors

Description:

An exciting team-based fun game designed to test speed, coordination, and teamwork! A word/phrase is given to the first member, and must be passed only through actions (no speech/devices). Teams of 4–5, 5 mins total. Break rules = elimination. Winners get gift cards!

About the Game:

  • Team members should stand one after another in an line.
  • A word or statement is passed to the first person in the line.
  • They should convey the word or statement in action.

Rules & Regulations:

  • Team of 4 to 5 members.
  • Total Allocation time per team is 5 min
  • 1 Min is allocated to pass the work through actions.
  • Should not convey the word or statement in oral form
  • Shouldn't use any electric and electronic devices in the game.
  • If any Team Members break the rules team will be eliminate.

Gift card is Provided For Winners

Requirements:

  • Enthusiasm and teamwork!
